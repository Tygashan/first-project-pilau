zeeshan

kenyan's favourite food

ingredients

  • basmat rice
  • chicken
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper
  • 5 cups of water
  • tomato paste
  • 1 onions
  • pilau masala
  • cooking oil

recipe

  1. Using a sufuria, heat oil and then add the onions Fry until the onions start to turn golden brown
  2. Add garlic and ginger and stir
  3. Add the chicken, pilau masala,black pepper and salt to taste. Cook until the meat browns
  4. Add tomato paste and stir occasionally for about 4 minutes
  5. Add the chicken and cook for 10 minutes bringing to a boil
  6. Stir in the basmat rice, and cover tightly with lid to keep the steam in
  7. Dice onions, and tomatoes and add a sprinkle of salt to make kachumbari.
  8. serve when warm with a drink(cold soda)
